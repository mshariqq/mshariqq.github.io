Questions I Get Asked A Lot

If yours isn't here, just message me.

Do you work with international clients? +
Yes — most of my clients are based outside India. I work across US, UK, UAE, and Australian timezones. Communication is in English and I'm responsive on WhatsApp and email.
What's your typical project timeline? +
A WordPress custom theme takes 1–2 weeks. A Shopify store setup takes 5–10 days. Larger web apps or dashboards are 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. I'll give you an exact timeline in the proposal.
Why custom WordPress theme instead of Elementor or Divi? +
Page builders add thousands of lines of bloated code that slow your site down, cause plugin conflicts, and break on updates. A custom theme is built exactly for your needs — nothing extra, nothing unnecessary. The result is a faster, cleaner, more maintainable site.
Do you provide post-launch support? +
Every project comes with 2 weeks of free post-launch support. After that, I offer monthly maintenance packages. I don't disappear after delivery.
What's your payment process? +
50% upfront to start, 50% on final delivery. For larger projects I use milestone-based payments. I work via Upwork for international clients (protected payments) or direct bank/UPI for Indian clients.
Can you work with my existing website? +
Yes — I do redesigns, migrations, speed optimization, and feature additions on existing WordPress and Shopify sites too. Just share the details and I'll assess what needs to be done.
